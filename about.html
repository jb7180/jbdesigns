About JB Designs


Interested in art and design from a young age, Julianne
has found a way to pursue her passions through JB
Designs. Julianne has a BA in Communications Studies
and is currently furthering her education in art and
design. Combining her knowledge of communication
and art, she is able to understand the intricacies of
branding, visuals, and creativity.
